Event Details

The city of Woodstock, NY is preparing to start a new open concert series, SNHU-a-palooza. The concerts will run on the first Friday of every month in a farmer’s field. Admission is free, but to protect the venue—and because past larger crowds damaged the farm—we’re capping attendance at 50,000 guests. If you’d like to join us under the stars, please register in advance!
